## 2. The Plasmoid as a Coherent Physical Entity

### 2.1 Definition and Properties

A plasmoid is a self-organized electromagnetic structure where internal currents generate closed magnetic topologies. In FRC plasmoids, axial field reversal produces a toroidal configuration without internal coils, minimizing material constraints.

### 2.2 Collision-Based Compression

Helion-style architectures accelerate two FRC plasmoids toward axial collision. Conservation of magnetic flux intensifies fields, raising density and temperature into fusion-relevant regimes.
